What We Treat

Common Conditions We Help Patients Manage

Back & Neck Pain

Chronic or acute pain from posture, injury, or disc issues.

Headaches & Migraines

Tension and cervicogenic headaches traced to spinal misalignment.

Joint Stiffness

Reduced range of motion in the shoulders, hips, knees, or spine.

Pinched Nerve

Numbness, tingling, or radiating pain from nerve compression.

Fibromyalgia

Widespread muscle pain and tenderness managed with gentle, ongoing care.

Sciatica

Sharp or burning pain radiating down the leg from the lower back.
