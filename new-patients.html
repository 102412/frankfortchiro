First Visit

What to Expect

1. Schedule

Request an appointment online or call your preferred office — most new patients are seen within a few days.

2. Intake & History

Complete intake paperwork (or submit it online beforehand) covering your health history and current symptoms.

3. Exam & Diagnosis

Your doctor performs a hands-on exam, and orders X-ray or EMG/NCV testing if needed to pinpoint the cause.

4. Treatment Plan

You leave with a personalized plan — and, in many cases, your first treatment the same day.

Insurance & Billing

Coverage Made Simple

We accept and bill most major insurances directly, including:

  • Medicare
  • Medicaid
  • VA Fee-for-Service
  • Personal Injury
  • Workers’ Compensation
  • Most Major Plans

We handle insurance verification and claims directly with your provider, so you don’t have to chase down paperwork. If you have questions about your specific plan, call either office and our staff will check your benefits before your visit.
