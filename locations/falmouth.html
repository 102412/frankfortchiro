What We Offer in Falmouth

Falmouth Chiropractic Center has been serving Falmouth and all of Pendleton County since 2007. Our services include full chiropractic care, manual adjustments, rehabilitation, therapeutic exercise, passive procedures, and a full circuit of strengthening equipment.

Doctors At This Location

Meet the Falmouth Team

Jason W. Luking, D.C.

Practice network owner · Founded Falmouth Chiropractic Center in 2007

Dr. Luking graduated from Palmer College of Chiropractic in 2000 and has been trained in needle EMG and NCV examinations since 2004. He founded Falmouth Chiropractic Center in 2007 to bring high-quality chiropractic care to Pendleton County. He is also the owner of the broader practice network.

Bryan Walther, D.C.

Licensed Kentucky chiropractor since 2008

Dr. Walther has been a licensed Kentucky chiropractor since 2008. He joined Falmouth Chiropractic Center and has been treating patients in Falmouth and Pendleton County ever since. He specializes in manual adjustments, rehabilitation, and therapeutic exercise, helping patients restore function and get back to living their lives.
