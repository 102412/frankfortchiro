Meet the Frankfort East Team

Beth Roth, D.C.

Joined January 2026

Dr. Roth earned a B.S. in Exercise and Sports Science from Coastal Carolina University in 2016 and her Doctor of Chiropractic from Palmer College of Chiropractic–Florida in 2019. She is Webster certified and has a particular passion for treating pregnant women and young children. Dr. Roth is a native of Lexington, KY.

Kyle Sutherland, D.C.

Licensed Chiropractor at Frankfort East since May 2022

Dr. Sutherland joined Frankfort Chiropractic Center as an intern in May 2021. He earned his Bachelor's in Life Sciences from Logan University in St. Louis, MO in 2018 and completed his Doctor of Chiropractic in December 2021. He resides in Lawrenceburg, KY.
