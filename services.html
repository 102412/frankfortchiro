Services & Conditions Treated

From chronic back pain to sports injuries, our doctors combine hands-on adjustments with modern diagnostic and rehab tools to treat the cause, not just the symptom.

What We Treat

Conditions We Help Patients Manage

Back & Neck Pain

Chronic or acute pain stemming from posture, injury, or disc issues. Our doctors identify the root cause through exam and, when needed, on-site imaging.

Headaches & Migraines

Tension and cervicogenic headaches are often traced back to spinal misalignment in the neck and upper back.

Joint Stiffness

Reduced range of motion in the shoulders, hips, knees, or spine, treated with adjustment and targeted rehab exercise.

Pinched Nerve

Numbness, tingling, or radiating pain caused by nerve compression — diagnosable on-site at Frankfort West with EMG/NCV testing.

Fibromyalgia

Widespread muscle pain and tenderness managed with gentle adjustments, massage, and an ongoing care plan.

Sciatica

Sharp or burning pain radiating down the leg from the lower back, often relieved through traction and adjustment.

How We Treat It

Treatment Modalities

Hands-On & Gentle Adjusting

Spinal manipulation tailored to each patient's health status and history — never a one-size-fits-all technique.

Dry Needling Therapy

Fine filament needle treatment for trigger points that reduces muscle tension and stimulates healing at the source.

Massage Therapy

Complements chiropractic care by improving muscle tone, relieving tension, and supporting circulation and alignment.

Rehabilitation & Adjunct Treatments

EMS, ultrasound, hot/cold treatment, intersegmental traction, TENS, and stretching/strengthening exercises in-office and at home.

Traction (Spinal Decompression)

A belt/pulley system decompresses disc spaces for lumbar and cervical conditions, with weight and angle set precisely per patient.

Digital X-Ray Services

On-site imaging for red-flag symptoms, scoliosis, spondylolisthesis, trauma evaluation, and degenerative changes.

West Only

EMG / NCV Testing

Nerve function testing performed on-site at Frankfort West by Dr. Luking using a Cadwell Sierra Summit machine.
