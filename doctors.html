Our Doctors

Four doctors of chiropractic, over 100 years of combined experience, across our two Frankfort locations.

Frankfort West

103 Twin Oaks Circle

Jason W. Luking, D.C.

Practice owner since early 2005 · Frankfort West

Dr. Luking attended Georgetown College before graduating from Palmer College of Chiropractic in 2000. He has been trained in needle EMG and NCV examinations since 2004 and has participated in professional peer review since 2008. He is married to Dr. Rebecca Luking, a family physician practicing in Frankfort.

Visit Frankfort West

Nathan Ritchie, D.C.

With the practice since 2012 · Frankfort West

Dr. Ritchie is a graduate of Harrison County High School in Kentucky. He earned a Bachelor's degree in Exercise Science from Georgetown College and his Doctorate of Chiropractic from Logan College of Chiropractic in St. Louis, MO. His special interests include sports injuries, nutrition, and functional movement, and he is certified in Dry Needling and Internal Health. Dr. Ritchie resides in Lawrenceburg with his wife Lindsay and their two sons.

Frankfort East

201 Brighton Park Blvd #4

Beth Roth, D.C.

Joined January 2026 · Frankfort East

Dr. Roth earned a B.S. in Exercise and Sports Science from Coastal Carolina University in 2016 and her Doctor of Chiropractic from Palmer College of Chiropractic–Florida in 2019. She is Webster certified and has a particular passion for treating pregnant women and young children. Dr. Roth is a native of Lexington, KY.

Kyle Sutherland, D.C.

Licensed Chiropractor at Frankfort East since May 2022
