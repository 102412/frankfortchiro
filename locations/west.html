Specialty Service

On-Site EMG / NCV Testing

Frankfort West is the only Frankfort Chiropractic location offering EMG (electromyography) and NCV (nerve conduction velocity) testing, performed on-site by Dr. Luking using a Cadwell Sierra Summit machine. This diagnostic testing helps pinpoint nerve damage and guide treatment for pinched nerves, sciatica, and numbness or tingling in the arms and legs.

Meet the Frankfort West Team

Jason W. Luking, D.C.

Practice owner since early 2005

Dr. Luking attended Georgetown College before graduating from Palmer College of Chiropractic in 2000. He has been trained in needle EMG and NCV examinations since 2004 and has participated in professional peer review since 2008. He is married to Dr. Rebecca Luking, a family physician practicing in Frankfort.

Nathan Ritchie, D.C.

With the practice since 2012

Dr. Ritchie is a graduate of Harrison County High School in Kentucky. He earned a Bachelor's degree in Exercise Science from Georgetown College and his Doctorate of Chiropractic from Logan College of Chiropractic in St. Louis, MO. His special interests include sports injuries, nutrition, and functional movement, and he is certified in Dry Needling and Internal Health. Dr. Ritchie resides in Lawrenceburg with his wife Lindsay and their two sons.
